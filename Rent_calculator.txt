The rent price depends on the initial price of the car, mileage, tenant's driving experience and the duration of the contract.

Initial monthly rent value is 2% of the car market price.

Final monthly rent value is calculated is follows: initial_price * 0,02 * driving_experience_coefficient * contract_length_coefficient * mileage_coefficient

Coefficients list:

Driving experience:	
less than 5:	1
5-10: 0,9
10-15: 0,8
more than 15: 0,7

Mileage:
7000: 1
14000: 0,9
21000:	0,8
28000:	0,7

Contract length:
12: 1
24: 0,9
36: 0,8
48: 0,7
